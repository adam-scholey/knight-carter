Who We Are

A Different Kind of Property Agency

Knight & Carter was founded on a simple belief: that every property transaction deserves the highest level of personal care and professional expertise. In a market often defined by volume, we have built our reputation on doing less — but doing it exceptionally well.

We are specialists in residential asset management across Yorkshire, working with homeowners, landlords and investors who expect more from their property partner. Our clients benefit from honest advice, transparent fees and a team who is fully invested in their outcome.

Whether you are buying your first home, selling a long-held family property, or growing a residential investment portfolio, Knight & Carter brings the knowledge and dedication to deliver results.
