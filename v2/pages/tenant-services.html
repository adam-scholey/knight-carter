Your Tenancy

Rent, Rights & Responsibilities

Everything you need to know about paying rent and what to expect during your tenancy.

01

Rent Payments

Rent is collected by standing order on the date agreed in your tenancy agreement. Contact us if you anticipate any difficulty making a payment — we're here to help, not to catch you out.

02

Your Rights

You have the right to a safe, well-maintained home, protection of your deposit in a government-approved scheme, and reasonable notice before any inspection or visit.

03

Your Responsibilities

Please pay rent on time, report repairs promptly, keep the property reasonably clean, and let us know about any changes to who is living at the property.
