Who We Are

A Different Kind of Letting Agency

Carter & Knight was founded on a simple belief: that every tenancy deserves the highest level of personal care and professional expertise. In a lettings market often defined by volume, we have built our reputation on doing less — but doing it exceptionally well.

We are specialists in residential lettings and property management, working with tenants and landlords who expect more from their letting agent. Our clients benefit from honest advice, transparent fees and a team who is fully invested in their outcome.

Whether you are searching for your next rental home or growing a residential letting portfolio, Carter & Knight brings the knowledge and dedication to deliver results.
