Compliance

Landlord Responsibilities

As a landlord, you're responsible for keeping the property safe and in good repair, ensuring valid gas and electrical safety certificates, protecting the tenant's deposit, and providing an Energy Performance Certificate.

Once your tenancy is live, rent collection and day-to-day maintenance are handled directly between you and your tenant. We're here to help you get set up right — from listing the property to signing the tenancy agreement.
