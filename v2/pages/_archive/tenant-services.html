Applying to Rent

How to Apply

Found a property you like? Arrange a viewing through the listing or our contact page, and if it's the right fit, we'll guide you through referencing, right-to-rent checks and signing the tenancy agreement.

Once your tenancy begins, your landlord becomes your main point of contact for the property — including day-to-day maintenance and repairs. Carter & Knight's role is to help you find the right home and get your tenancy set up smoothly.

Your Tenancy

Rent, Rights & Responsibilities

Everything you need to know about paying rent and what to expect during your tenancy.

01

Rent Payments

Rent is paid by standing order directly to your landlord on the date agreed in your tenancy agreement. Contact us if you're unsure who to pay once your tenancy is confirmed.

02

Your Rights

You have the right to a safe, well-maintained home, protection of your deposit in a government-approved scheme, and reasonable notice before any inspection or visit.

03

Repairs & Maintenance

Maintenance is handled directly by your landlord once your tenancy begins. Your tenancy agreement will include their contact details for reporting any issues with the property.
