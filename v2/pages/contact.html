For Tenants

Common Tenant Questions

Quick answers to the essentials before you get in touch.

How do I apply to rent a property?

Arrange a viewing through the listing or our contact form. If the property is right for you, we will guide you through referencing, right-to-rent checks and signing the tenancy agreement.

How is my deposit protected?

Your tenancy deposit is protected in a government-approved deposit protection scheme. This is a legal requirement and we will provide you with the scheme details when your tenancy is confirmed.

How do I report a repair?

Once your tenancy begins, your landlord is your main point of contact for maintenance and repairs. Their details will be included in your tenancy agreement. Contact us if you need help reaching them.

Who do I pay rent to?

Rent is paid by standing order directly to your landlord on the date agreed in your tenancy agreement. We can confirm the payment details once your tenancy is set up.
